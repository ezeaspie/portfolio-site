About Me

I started learing Web Development in 2016, starting by building simple HTML pages in Notepad. I then learned some CSS and then discovered the power of Javascript over the next few months - enabling me to build things beyond simple static pages.

Once I had the basics down, I didn't stop and kept pushing to learn whatever I could to become a better developer. By learning to use different frameworks, libraries, and cutting edge features of the languages, I know that there is always something else to learn that can take me to the next level.

I mostly work with the front-end, specializing in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. When designing pages and applications, I take time to plan and create designs to speed up the process. I also take accessibility, responsiveness, and user-experience into mind during the process.

Outside of Web Development, I enjoy writing, creating art, watching soccer games, and maintaining beautiful aquariums.
